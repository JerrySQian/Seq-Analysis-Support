Support

Support Information

This app was created to provide an easy way to determine whether DNA (RNA sequence analysis is coming soon!) is relatively stable or unstable, along with a confidence value. As of right now, this app is only available for iOS. If there is a problem running this app on your iOS device, please use the contact information below and report your issue. You may expect to recieve a response within 48 hours, although response times may differ during holidays and summer.

Have questions about Seq Analysis for iOS's privacy policy? Visit the privacy policy page here.       

If you have further questions about using this app or general questions pertaining to the app, please use the email listed below to contact the developer. 

Bug Reporting

This app was extensively tested to ensure convenience. Through this testing, we have modeled a layout for a seamless user experience. This app should be easy to use, intuitive, and have an elegant design.    

If you would like to report a bug, please contact the developer using the email listed below in CONTACT. Please put "Seq Analysis App Bug" as the subject.  

If you would like to leave a review of this app, please do so on the App Store app located on your iOS device.
